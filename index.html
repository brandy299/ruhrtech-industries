About RuhrTech Industries

RuhrTech Industries GmbH is a German special machine engineering company based in Essen. We are a GmbH, a German limited liability company.

We design and make CNC milling machines, assembly lines, and test stands for the automotive industry. We sell to clients around the world.

Our Mission

We build machines that make automotive production faster, more precise, and more efficient. Engineering excellence, made in Germany.

  • ● Business areas: CNC milling machines, assembly lines, and test stands
  • ● Clients: Tier 1 and tier 2 automotive suppliers around the world
  • ● Markets: Europe, Asia, and North America
  • ● Certifications: ISO 9001, ISO 14001, IATF 16949

Working at RuhrTech Industries

We are growing and looking for new team members at our Essen headquarters.

Junior Industrial Clerk

Essen HQ · Full-time · Industriekaufmann/-frau

We are looking for motivated Junior Industrial Clerks to join our Essen team. You will work in different departments like Sales, Purchasing, Logistics, and HR.

Your Tasks:

  • ▸ Process customer orders and supplier inquiries
  • ▸ Prepare quotations and purchase orders
  • ▸ Help with logistics and shipping to international customers
  • ▸ Update the ERP system and customer database
  • ▸ Communicate with suppliers and customers in English

What We Offer:

  • ✓ Modern office in Essen with good public transport connections
  • ✓ International team with English as a working language
  • ✓ Training programme and career development
  • ✓ 30 days of holiday and flexible working hours
  • ✓ Travel opportunities to our offices in Shanghai and Detroit
