RuhrTech
INDUSTRIES
GMBH

RuhrTech Industries GmbH is a German special machine engineering company based in Essen, NRW. Constituted as a GmbH under German law, we specialise in CNC milling centres, automated assembly lines, and QA test platforms for the automotive supply sector.

Our three international facilities — Essen (HQ), Shanghai, and Detroit — serve tier 1 and tier 2 automotive suppliers across European, Asian, and North American markets.

MISSION

Engineer machinery that optimises throughput, precision, and operational efficiency. German engineering — globally deployed.
